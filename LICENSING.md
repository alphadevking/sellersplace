# Commercial Licensing

SellersPlace is **source-available, not open source** — the code is public so
you can evaluate exactly what you'd be running, but operating it as a live
store requires a commercial license from **Nexhub Labs**. See [LICENSE](LICENSE)
for the full terms.

## Who this is for

SellersPlace exists so small businesses can put their products and services
online — with payments, orders, delivery tracking, support chat, and SEO built
in — without paying big-website or big-hosting money. Each deployment is
re-branded per business (name, logo, colors, catalog vocabulary) from
configuration alone.

## Standard license (per store)

One commercial license covers **one business, one store, one domain** and
includes:

- Full deployment and production use of the software for your store
- Your branding throughout (name, logo, brand color, hero media, catalog)
- Setup: domain, database, Paystack keys, and initial catalog load
- Updates and fixes as the template improves

The **"Built by Nexhub Labs"** credit remains part of the standard license.

## White-label

Removal of the developer credit is available as a separate white-label
upgrade, agreed in writing.

## What you may not do (with or without a standard license)

- Resell, redistribute, or republish the code or a derivative as a template,
  theme, boilerplate, or hosted platform
- Operate stores for third parties without a license per store
- Remove attribution outside a white-label agreement
